Who We Are

The BUS Party Youth Ambassadors is a grassroots political and civic movement established to empower the youth of Embu County to actively participate in governance, leadership, and community development. The movement operates under the values and vision of the BUS Party, serving as a bridge between young people and the party's political agenda.

We believe that meaningful change begins with informed, organized, and empowered youth. Through structured leadership, mentorship, and grassroots mobilization, we nurture a generation of disciplined, ethical, and visionary leaders ready to shape the future of Embu County and beyond.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower the youth of Embu County to actively participate in political, social, and economic development through leadership training, grassroots mobilization, mentorship, and strategic engagement.

Our Vision

To build a united, vibrant, and influential youth movement that drives positive change, strengthens democratic participation, and produces future leaders committed to inclusive governance and community prosperity.

Our Core Values

Inclusivity

We embrace diversity and ensure equal opportunities for all youth regardless of background or circumstance.

Accountability

We uphold transparency, integrity, and responsibility in leadership and decision-making processes.

Leadership

We nurture responsible, visionary leaders who serve with purpose, discipline, and ethical standards.

Service

We are committed to community impact, civic responsibility, and sustainable development.

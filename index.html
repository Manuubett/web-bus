About the Movement

The BUS Party Youth Ambassadors is a grassroots movement established to empower young people across Embu County to actively participate in leadership, governance, and community development. We serve as a bridge between the youth and the BUS Party, ensuring youth voices are heard and transformed into action.

Our Core Objectives

Youth Mobilization

Recruit, organize, and engage youth across wards, institutions, and communities.

Leadership Development

Identify, mentor, and elevate young leaders into party and public leadership roles.

Good Governance

Promote transparency, accountability, and ethical political participation.

Community Impact

Drive social, economic, and civic initiatives that directly benefit communities.
